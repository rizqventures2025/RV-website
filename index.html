Our Guiding Principles

Surah Al-Jumu'ah (62:10)

فَإِذَا قُضِيَتِ الصَّلَاةُ فَانتَشِرُوا فِي الْأَرْضِ وَابْتَغُوا مِن فَضْلِ اللَّهِ

"When the prayer is finished, disperse in the land and seek the bounty of Allah."

**Takeaway:** Worship comes first, but believers are commanded to work and seek Allah's provision through *halal* means. Deen and dunya go hand in hand.

Hadith (Sahih al-Bukhari 1427)

الْيَدُ الْعِلْيَا خَيْرٌ مِنَ الْيَدِ السُّفْلَى...

"The upper hand (giver) is better than the lower hand (taker)..."

**Takeaway:** True *barakah* lies in providing for others after fulfilling our own needs. Rizq Ventures helps youth become self-reliant givers in society, not seekers.

About Rizq Ventures

Organization Overview

Rizq Ventures is a purpose-driven platform empowering individuals, startups, traditional businesses, and institutes through **mentorship, incubation, digitization, and strategic support**. Backed by young professionals and industry experts, we foster sustainable growth and community impact centered on *halal* livelihoods.

Our Mission

Empower individuals and communities to thrive with **purpose and professionalism**. We nurture excellence across education, entrepreneurship, and empowerment within the community.

Our Vision

To build a generation that excels in both **deen and dunya** through structured mentorship, knowledge dissemination, and entrepreneurial support.

Organization Model

  • Key Goals:
  • Empower Individuals (Youth Excellence)
  • Drive Excellence (Scholar/Educator Support)
  • Foster Community (Startup & SME Incubation)
  • Operating Model:
  • **Volunteer Powered** and Community Driven Governance.
  • **Self Sustainable** financial model.
  • Monthly review and progress tracking.

Our Programs & Pillars

YEP

Youth Excellence Program: Structured Mentorship

**Purpose:** To help young individuals align their career growth and personal development with Islamic values and professional excellence.

  • **Format:** Cohort-based mentorship programs (online/offline).
  • **Activities:** Naseeha sessions, retreats, community events, and guest speakers.

ThinkSeed

Expert-Led Courses & Educator Platform

**Purpose:** To enable scholars, educators, and experts to efficiently launch and scale their authentic courses.

  • **Support:** End-to-end course hosting, platform support, marketing, and monetization strategies.

Incubate Rizq

Startup & SME Support

**Purpose:** To help Muslim-led startups and traditional businesses move from idea to scale, ensuring stability and sustainability.

  • **Focus Areas:** Early-stage ideation to MVP, branding, strategy, tech stack, and go-to-market.
